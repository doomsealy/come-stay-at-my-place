Meet Marlon "Bigg Slim"

Saint Lucian DJ & Entertainment Specialist Since 2009

From intimate weddings to massive carnival celebrations, Marlon brings 15+ years of experience and an unmatched passion for creating the perfect atmosphere for every occasion.
